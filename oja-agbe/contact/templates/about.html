About Oja Agbe Harvestify

Oja Agbe Harvestify is a transformative platform designed to connect farmers with consumers and buyers seamlessly. Our mission is to empower the agricultural community with fair trade opportunities and cutting-edge tools to optimize their operations.

We leverage technology to bridge the gap between farmers and markets, fostering sustainable agriculture and enriching rural communities.

Our Vision

To revolutionize agriculture and build a more equitable and sustainable food system for everyone.

Our Mission

To empower farmers by providing them with innovative tools and resources while connecting them directly with buyers for maximum profitability and efficiency.

Our Core Values

  • Sustainability: Promoting eco-friendly and sustainable farming practices.
  • Innovation: Utilizing technology to drive agricultural growth.
  • Fair Trade: Ensuring farmers receive fair compensation for their work.

Our Achievements

  • Over 10,000 farmers onboarded in the first year.
  • Recognized as a top innovative startup in agricultural technology in 2024.
  • Facilitated over 1 million direct transactions between farmers and buyers.
